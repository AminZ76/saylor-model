How the model works

Projection engine

All variables use exponential trend extrapolation with lognormal confidence intervals, computed in quarterly steps. Bands widen at longer horizons, correctly representing compounding uncertainty.

V(t) = V₀ × (1 + r_q)^t × exp(±nσ√t)

MSTR mNAV model

MSTR price derives from modified Net Asset Value. The mNAV premium peaked at ~4× in late 2024 and has compressed to ~1.2× as competing Bitcoin treasury companies entered the market.

Price = (BTC × BTC_price × mNAV) ÷ shares

M2 liquidity coupling

The BTC growth rate incorporates a liquidity coupling factor. At 0, BTC moves independently. At 1, BTC fully absorbs M2 growth as additional return. Research suggests a 60–70 day lag.

r_eff = r_base + coupling × r_M2

Confidence bands

±1σ bands cover ~68% of outcomes given the assumptions. ±2σ bands cover ~95%. Bitcoin's historical annual volatility of 50–80% makes the ±2σ price band very wide — this is accurate, not a flaw.

±1σ → 68% | ±2σ → 95%
